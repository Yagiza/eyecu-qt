1. Press «Open registration website» to open registration page in your system web browser.
  2. Type desired user name into «Nutzerkennung(*):» field.
  3. Type desired password into «Passwort(*):» field.
  4. Enter your e-mail address into «Email-Adressse(*):» field.
  5. Optionally enter your real name (First, Last) into «Vor-/Nachname:» field.
  6. Your e-mail and real name will be used for user search.
  7. Check «Ich habe die Nutzungsbedinungen gelesen und stimme diesen zu.» checkbox.
  8. Enter text from the picture into the field under the picture.
  9. Press «Anmelden» button to proceed registration.
  10. If registatration is successful, close your web browser, or try different user name otherwise.
  11. Press «Next» button below to proceed to credentials page.
