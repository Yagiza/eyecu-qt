1. Press «Open registration website» to open registration page in your system web browser.
  2. Type desired user name into «Username:» field.
  3. Select appropriate domain (%1) under «Jabber domain:» title.
  4. Type desired password into «Password:» field.
  5. Retype password into «Verify password:» field.
  6. Type your e-mail addedss into «E-mail (optional):» field, if you like. You may use it later to recover your password if you forget it.
  7. Type into «Captcha:» field text from the picture below.
  8. Press «CREATE ACCOUNT» button to finish registration.
  9. If registatration is successful, close your web browser, or correct errors and try again otherwise.
  10. Press «Next» button below to proceed to credentials page.
