1. Press «Open registration website» to open registration page in your system web browser.
  2. Type your e-mail into «email» field.
  3. Type desired user name into «Username» field. If "Username" title became dark red, try different username, until green "Username is still available," message appears below.
  4. Type image from the picture under «Captcha:» title into field below.
  5. Press «Register» button to proceed registration.
  6. After you see «Thanks for your registration!» message, check the mailbox, specified at step 2.
  7. You should receive a message with subject «Your new account on jabber.at». Open it.
  8. Click «click here» link to open «Confirm Registration» page.
  9. Type desired password into «Password» field.
  10. Retype password into «Confirm» field.
  11. Type image from the picture under «Captcha:» title into field below.
  12. Press «Confirm» button to finish registration.
  13. If registatration is successful, close your web browser, or correct errors and try again otherwise.
  14. Press «Next» button below to proceed to credentials page.
