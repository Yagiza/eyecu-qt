1. Press «Open web page» to open registration page in your system web browser.
  2. Fill in the registration form:
    1. Enter your First and Last name into «Name» fields.
    2. Enter your username into «Choose your username» field.
    3. Type desired password into «Create a password» field.
    4. Retype your password into «Confirm your password» field.
    5. Specify your birthday in «Birthday» fields.
    6. Specify your gender into «Gender» field.
    7. Enter text from the picture under «Prove you're not a robot» label into «Type the text» field.
    8. Set «I agree to the Google Terms of Service and Privacy Policy» checkmark.
    9. Optionally, you may specify Mobile phone and Your current e-mail address in corresponding fields.
  3. Press «Next step» button to proceed registration.
  4. If you see «Create your public Google+ profile» page, your registration finished successfuly. Otherwise, correct problems in the data you filled in form with and try again.
  5. If registatration is successful, you may go on and add your photo by clicking «Add a photo» link, create your public Google+ account by pressing «Create your profile» button, if you like, or just close your web browser to contunue.
  6. Press «Next» button below to proceed to connecion page.
